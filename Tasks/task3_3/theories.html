Theories About the End of the Universe

Home  |  Theories

1. Heat Death (Big Freeze)

The most accepted theory. The universe keeps expanding until all stars die out. No energy is left, and everything becomes cold and dark forever.

2. The Big Crunch

The opposite of the Big Bang. Gravity stops expansion and pulls everything back to a single point — possibly starting another Big Bang.

3. The Big Rip

Dark energy increases so much that it tears apart galaxies, stars, planets, and even atoms. The universe "rips" itself apart.
