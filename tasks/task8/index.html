About the Museum

The Grand Egyptian Museum houses the world's largest collection of ancient Egyptian artifacts. A space where history, culture, and modern design come together at the foot of the Giza Pyramids.

Admission is currently limited to the Main Galleries, Grand Hall, Grand Staircase, commercial area, and exterior gardens. Access to the Tutankhamun galleries and Khufu's Boats Museum is restricted until the official opening.

Please note that this is the only official website for purchasing tickets to the GEM. The museum is not responsible for tickets bought from other platforms.
